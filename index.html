puff pastry blueberry Galette

source: Foodista

Description:

This is a delicious blueberry galette made with nice, flaky puff pastry and served with a sweet, tangy blueberry filling. Perfect for a dessert or snack.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of blueberries, fresh or frozen
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of milk or heavy cream
  • 1 sheet of puff pastry, thawed

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the blueberries, cornstarch, lemon juice, lemon zest, and sugar, then set aside.
  3. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the puff pastry, then trim it into a rough circle approximately 9 inches in diameter.
  4. Pile the blueberry mixture into the center of the pastry, then fold the edges in and around the filling.
  5. Brush the edges of the pastry with milk or heavy cream and sprinkle with sugar
  6. Bake for 30-45 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving.
  8. serve with ice cream or whipped cream as desired.
